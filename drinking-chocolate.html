Make Your Cup

Your Café Ritual Begins Here.

Rich café-style drinking chocolate in four beautifully simple steps.

01

Spoon

Add 2–3 teaspoons to your favourite mug.

02

Mix

Stir with a splash of warm milk until smooth.

03

Whisk

Add hot milk and whisk until rich and velvety.

04

Savour

Enjoy as it is or finish with marshmallows, whipped cream or chocolate shavings.

Prefer it cold?

Make a smooth chocolate paste, add chilled milk, pour over ice and stir.
