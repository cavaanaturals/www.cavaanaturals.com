Not Just Hot Chocolate.

This is chocolate made for slowing down.

Cavaa Signature Drinking Chocolate brings the warmth and indulgence of your favourite café into your home.

Richer and more comforting than an everyday cocoa drink, it creates a smooth, satisfying cup with deep chocolate character and beautifully balanced sweetness.

Whisk it into warm milk for a classic cup, pour it over ice, blend it into a milkshake, or transform it into your favourite chocolate dessert.

```
Make Your Cup

Your Café Moment, Made at Home.

Create a rich, comforting cup in just a few simple steps. Adjust the quantity to make it exactly as indulgent as you like.

01

Spoon

Add 2 to 3 teaspoons of Cavaa Signature Drinking Chocolate to your favourite mug.

02

Pour

Add a small splash of warm milk and stir until the chocolate forms a smooth paste.

03

Whisk

Pour in approximately 150 to 180 ml of hot milk and whisk until rich, smooth, and velvety.

04

Savour

Top with marshmallows, whipped cream, chocolate shavings, or simply enjoy it beautifully plain.

For an iced chocolate

Prepare a smooth chocolate paste with a little warm milk, add chilled milk, pour over ice, and stir well.

```
